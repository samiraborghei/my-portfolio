Hi I'm Samira

I am a Web/Graphic Designer.

I am a multidimensional designer who passionate about good communication and collaboration with clients to facilitate creative problem solving. My skills and abilities include web motion, multimedia, photo editing, logo and brand identity design. I honed my creative skills during my seven years working as a graphic designer for a broad range of companies including Farsana Group LLC and FSN Corp.

Samira holds a B.A. in Graphic Design from Dr Shariaty University located in Iran, as well asgot two certificates in web design and web development from Northern Virginia Community College. Her wide range of talent and cross-cultural experience will benefit every position .

Art Philosophy

I love drawing for a number of reasons- for the challenge, for the joy of expression, for the feeling of pride in work I've done, for the sense of creation that is mine, for the excitement I feel when I get obsessed with a drawing- these are a few of the reasons that come to mind. Many of my favorite drawing have come from finding a image that I want to make my own. This may take the form of an image I've come across that I love or a photo I've taken.
